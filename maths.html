Decimal to Binary Converter

In computer science, numbers are often represented in binary (base 2). This tool converts a decimal (base 10) number into binary.



Why Binary is Important

Binary representation is essential in computing because computers store and process data using only two states: 0 and 1. Understanding binary helps explain how memory, logic, and digital systems work.
